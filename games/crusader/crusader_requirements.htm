Requirements

 

Format: (1) CD-ROM disk

Category: Action-Adventure

Computers: MS-DOS 5.0 or compatible, Intel 486/75+ MHz, Pentium™ or 100% compatible system with 2X CD-ROM

Sound Devices for Music: Sound Blaster

Sound Devices for Speech: Sound Blaster or 100% Compatible

Graphics Supported: 256-color SVGA (VESA compliant)

RAM and HD Storage Requirements: 8 megs RAM; 20 megs hard drive (Crusader 1); 65 megs hard drive (Crusader 2)

Playing time: 35+ hours
