The first thing that becomes obvious is that this is a total rip-off of Origin's Crusader: No Remorse. Same isometric view, huge explosions, even similar colors and artwork. The one twist is that in Bedlam, you get to pilot three separate heavily armored RATs (Remote Assault Tanks) in an attempt to wipe out the infestation. Speaking of the RAT you're directing, it's an interesting piece of machinery. Although it floats around the playfield without apparent feet or wheels, it's incapable of jumping off the smallest ledge. It can deal death with several kinds of weapons (outfitted in the handy, MechWarrior-esque weapon installation screen), but it can't hop off a two-foot tall overhang. Go figure.

-Gamezilla
