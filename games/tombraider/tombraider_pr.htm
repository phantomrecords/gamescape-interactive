 - Run - Shoot - Swim -

Your Way Through Tomb Raider because ya just gotta~!

Now I'm not in the habit of hyping up games but I'll certainly hype this one~! That's because the demo was surprisingly fun. It all started on a Saturday night after I saw the commercial. I figured "what a silly name" but then I thought "I don't have a social life either." So I jumped on the Internet, typed in a search and wham... I was at www.TombRaider.com and the demo was downloading.

It started off a bit confusing but naturally hadn't read the instructions. Does anyone? It took some time but after pressing F1 to get the screen res high and SPACE to draws the guns, everything was cool!

Lara, the chick in the flics, is quite on to it! She'll aim her guns at whatever is a threat. That's her way of telling the player "fire you imbecile or I'll get clobbered!"

She'll also think about her surroundings before she reacts. For example there's a part in the demo where she gets stuck in a room. There's a stone cube in the wall that must be moveable somehow. But how?

No buttons, no switches, there ain't nothing... Just you and the stone. So I started pressing the CTRL key while facing the door... and then CTRL-UP and pouf! Without a pause, Lara hunched down, got in a cool stance, and threw her weight at the stone, heaving and pushing bit by bit until it gave way. The sound effects were cool and I was free!

But wait there's more...

Swimming is definitely the best... The developers animated it so well that I want to say things like "dead on" and "awesome" but I might sound like a TV ad. Can't have that so I'll let the demo speak for itself... I trust you'll see where I'm coming from once you've played it!

Ta...!
