Playability:

Everything is mouse driven, including traveling and fighting. There are keyboard shortcuts for all useful commands. You can choose between three characters, Fighter, Sorcerer, and Rogue. Those first two character classes are fairly self-explanatory. The last, the Rogue, may sound like a thief, but is somewhere between

thief and ranger, for those of you into Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. In fact, Diablo is very similar to AD&D's rules. You have attributes, such as Strength, Dexterity, and Magic Resistance, to name a few. You advance levels by killing monsters, and get points to increase those attributes as you see fit. Decide carefully, because once you choose, that's it.
