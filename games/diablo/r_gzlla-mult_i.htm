Battle.net is half the fun. Try to ignore immature PKers and thieves, because anything you lose can be found again. Keep abreast of recent events in the chat channel, and as soon as a higher level channels are available to you (levels 20+, 30+, and 40+), check them out. They tend to be more tranquil and less crowded, and the items for trade and topics of discussion are much more relevant. Finally, don't forget to have a good time, and possibly send email to Blizzard thanking them for one of the best games of all time.

-Gamezilla (Multiplay Review)
