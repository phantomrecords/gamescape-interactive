Gamesmania
Review
"Clandestiny is an adventure-style game that allows the gamer to walk through the ancient hallways of a Scottish castle. [Screen Shot 2] Clandestiny , set in present day Scotland, is centered around a Scottish laird and his cursed clan of the MacPhiles. Your character, Andrew MacPhiles [ Screen Shot 3], travels to Scotland to claim his title and his inheritance. [ Screen Shot 4] However, the cowardly Andrew does not realize that a curse has been put on his entire clan and that his inheritance will end up being his death if he cannot reclaim the past and destroy the curse."

Requirements

Windows 95, Pentium P-60, 8 Megabytes RAM, double speed (300k/sec) CD-ROM, local bus video with 1 MB of RAM capable of displaying thousands of colors and supports DirectX 2.0, 100% Sound Blaster compatible, Windows 95 operating system. Runs on all AST, Canon, Compaq, HP, IBM, NEC, and Packard Bell systems with preinstalled multimedia since 1/1/95. Recommended: mouse, good powered speakers.
