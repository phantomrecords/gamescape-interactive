Parties:

No, I am not talking about drunken bashes. If you want those, head for Ogden's Tavern. I am talking about multiplayer cooperative groups. When your group is decided, use the whisper command to tell your group members what the game name and password will be. Then push the "Create" button, which lets you create a new multiplayer game. Give the game a name and password (be careful, the password is case sensitive), and then go! If

another player is starting the game instead, push the "Join" button and enter the game name and password at the prompt.

If you've tried and can't seem to be able to join or muster up an adventurous group, simply choose "Join" and take a look at the current game list. This tells you what games are going on, what kind of lag they are showing, and who (what level, class, etc.) started the game. Pick a game with someone of similar level and double click the name of

the game. If you can enter the game, it will put you right in. However, if you get the notorious "Sorry, but you cannot enter this game" message, you have one of a number of problems. Either you need a password (or it is incorrect, check upper/lower case), have too much lag, are not high enough level to enter, or the game is full. Try your luck on another game. If you wait a few seconds, the list will scroll down to the next group of active games, and you can try your luck there.
