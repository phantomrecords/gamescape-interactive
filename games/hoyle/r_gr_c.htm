An added surprise is the "Card Flip" game. This is a mindless exercise involving flipping cards into different objects. Yet somehow this becomes addictive as well (maybe it was the fever or something). Overall, the Hoyle series manages to break through the humdrum cloud of PC card gaming by offering well- planned games. If you dig cards, then you should dig this.
