Requirements

Requirements: IBM and 100% compatibles, Microsoft DOS 6.0 or greater, 486/66 or better, 256 color VGA, VLB or PCI bus required, double speed CD-ROM drive or better, MPC level 2 requried, 8MB RAM, 10MB available hard drive space required for minimum install, Microsoft 100% compatible mouse required, joystick optional. Sound cards supported: Sound Blaster, Sound Blaster Pro, Sound Blaster 16, AWE 32, Pro Audio Spectrum, Ensoniq Soundscape, Gravis Ultrasound and 100% compatibles. Compatible in MS-DOS mode with sufficient free RAM. SVGA requirements: Pentium processor and 256 color 640x480 VESA compatible video card. Multiplayer requirements: 14.4 modem (28.8 recommended) or IPX or Net Bios compatible LAN.
