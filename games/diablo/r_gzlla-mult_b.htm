Group Adventures:

Once you feel you are ready to jump into the multiplayer world of Diablo, crank up your Internet connection and choose a new multiplayer game over Battle.net via the Diablo menu. Enter a chat room

(avoid the Town Square at first, it is mostly for trading) and look for a bunch of guys near your level that are looking for adventure. When you see a person's name, it will show you a picture (depicting their class), a number indicating their character's level, possibly a number of red dots over the individual's picture, and a series of green, yellow, or red bars.

The red dots (max 3) tell which difficulty levels an individual has killed Diablo on,

so eventually you are going to want to join the club. Beware of the colored bars to the right of an individual's name_1; They indicate how much lag a person has relative to you, so you would be better off playing with people that are in the green. Push F1 for a little primer on Battle.net and the chat interface, particularly for information on /msg, /squelch, /unsquelch, /whisper, and other commands. Once you've made a few friends, it's off to the depths of Hell!
