 Sept-12-96 REVIEW: What can I say? C&C is gameplay at its best! I'm not just blowin' smoke up your butt! It really is! Just you grab a copy and try to get away from it before 2 hours go by! Yea, I've heard it all before... "I don't like this and I don't like that"... and then you play it and make excuses for getting sucked in!


Command and Conquer

It's OK, it happens to us all. And with Command and Conquer a whole lot'a folks are making excuses for not doing the dishes, not wasing the car, forgetting to pick up the baby, and yep even forgetting to shower!! Don't believe it ya say? Only those looser guys who don't have jobs do that ya think? OK, I'll drop it off to your home on a boring Saturday morning, and let's see how close to the shower you get!

The folks at PC Gamer magazine had it nailed when they said "The more you play, the more you learn, and the more engrossed you become."

Command and Conquer gets 5 stars from this reviewer!
