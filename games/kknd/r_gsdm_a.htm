Krush Kill 'N' Destroy is set in the not too distant future. The bombs were launched, all out Nuclear War affected every part of the world's population. Now, 60 odd years later, two very distinct and different groups of people have been formed, each with their own conflicting beliefs and religion that forces what's left of human life back into war. The superb FMV computer-generated introduction tells the story well, complete with the melodramatic voice-over from the narrator. "And so... there is... WAR!" etc. I love it.

The Two Sides

The 'Survivors' are the scattered groups that managed to hide underground while the Earth's atmosphere was completely polluted with poisonous and radioactive gases. They stayed underground for 60 years, continually searching for resources to keep them alive, until just recently, where they are now starting to emerge to the surface.

 The other group, the 'Evolved', are the batch of humans that could not find sanctuary underground. Millions died, some survived, progressively mutating into gross-looking and very paranoid beings. They are majorly deprived of any sort of technology, and rely on training the also badly 'evolved' wildlife for their weapons. So their equivalent of the Survivor's 4x4 Pickup would be to mount a galloping mini-dinosaur type creature, or command huge threatening poison-spitting scorpions.
