Player Killers and Thieves:

These two are the

scourge of multiplayer games. Opinions vary on the value/honor of PKillers, but there is rarely any argument about thieves. PKillers are characters that hunt down other players (usually in cooperative games), and usually pick on unsuspecting characters weaker than them. While PKilling (or PKing for short) is not forbidden or even discouraged by Blizzard, most players are strongly against it. On the other hand, players that just want to try out their stuff will often agree with another player on a deathmatch game, with the stakes for the winner decided ahead of time. Deathmatching is very different from Pkilling due to the large degree of cowardice and dishonesty that is generally associated with the latter.
