Strategy/Tips:

 For those of you out there who can't wait for a hint book, here are a few suggestions that you might find useful. First, when starting the game, no matter what nationality you choose, CUSTOMIZE IT. You needn't change anything; just click OK to accept the default setting. The reason for this is that the game will usually provide you with up to three more technological advances than you would not otherwise have (the basics being the ability to build roads, mines, and irrigation). In the long haul you'll need every edge you can get over your rivals especially later in the game.

Remember to try and expand quickly in the beginning of the game. More cities means more revenue and higher production levels from your citizens.
