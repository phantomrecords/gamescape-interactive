Finally, the Rogue is a powerful character, somewhat of a blend of the other two classes, sharing some of the strengths and weaknesses of both.

If you want to make it big, you're going to have to mix it up. Never travel into difficult areas of the dungeon with only members of your

own class. Chances are, you will eventually run into a creature against which your attacks have little effect, and you will find yourself hoping for some different companionship. Instead, try to use parties of at least one character of each class, balancing each other out quite well. However, try to share treasure/experience equally, and be aware of the damage spells and ranged attacks can have on other players. It is not rare for a Sorcerer or Rogue cowering in the corner to pepper the Warrior (who is usually taking the front line) with arrows or fireballs, so remember to bring along a couple of resurrection spells just to show that there are no hard feelings.
