The tallest challenge, however, is taking care of the three different vehicles, on three different fronts, all at once. Switching among RATs, without getting the others blown to smithereens, takes quite a bit of practice. This is especially true when playing in Windows 95; it isn't quite as snappy speed-wise as the DOS version, something you may have already guessed. All in all, there are plenty of cool weapons and high-res

graphics to satisfy the most weary of action fans until the next Crusader comes out.
