92% PC Zone April 1996

With Spectrum's Top Gun there's a smooth blending of two completely different art forms: movie-style adventure with mid-action chatter. the In-flight chatter is both abundant and unobtrusive giving a real sense of being on the edge. The storyline is both cinematic, and nicely scripted. "Atmosphere, with a capital 'A'. It's completely interactive... And it actually works. Jesus Christ! Praise be to the lord!"
