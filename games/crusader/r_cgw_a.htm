What happens in the middle is the meat of the game. The mission environments are massive, yet they are constructed in such a way that you'll rarely get confused. That this can be done without it seeming like you're being led by the hand is quite a feat. Once in the mission, Crusader is a relatively simple game. Which is not to say it's easy. It's simple in that you can grasp the mechanics and nuances of the gameplay rather quickly, and they remain consistent throughout the play experience. -Computer Gaming World
