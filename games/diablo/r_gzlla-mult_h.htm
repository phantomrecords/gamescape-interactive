There is one other threat of theft, due to a bug that Blizzard has yet to fix. If a player loses their Internet connection, the current game usually stays active, but all other players drop out. As a result, some players will kill their net connection as soon as you put your item on the ground, thus kicking you out of their game and giving them a free item. This is a new kind of thievery, and is much harder to prevent. However, it is reassuring that in the higher levels, characters tend to be much more honest and dependable, so it becomes less of a problem with time.

Charity:

As you reach higher levels, you will find many rings and other such items that are inferior to those you are already using, and cash is available in abundance later in the game. As a result, it is usually best to give away such trinkets to lower level characters, since the items have little value to you and clog up your inventory. Other players will appreciate it, and

it is a nice way to counterbalance the occasional PKer or thief. In addition, you will find that other players are likely to do the same for you, giving away unneeded or outmoded equipment.
