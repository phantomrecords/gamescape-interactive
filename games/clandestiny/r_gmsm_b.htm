Storyline:

This adventure game is based on the cursed clan of the MacPhiles and you are the last living descendent that must destroy the hex and claim your legacy. Your character, Andrew MacPhiles, must travel from his homeland of America to the distant shores of Scotland. Unfortunately this plot has been over used throughout the years, but the comic take

might make it an intriguing adventure game to play. In sticking with tradition, Andrew MacPhiles is as cowardly as his descendants (this doesn't exactly make him exciting to play). He went to Scotland not to destroy the curse, but to clam his imaginary fortune. Andrew is accompanied by his girlfriend who is enthralled by the castle and the idea of marrying a Scottish Earl. To say the least, Paula is annoying and full of grand wedding plans, but she does add some much-needed comic relief. The other reoccurring characters of the butler, the handyman, the cook and the vicar all add historical facts about various Scottish clans and the surrounding regions. All of the characters do usually give humorous performances, although I'm sure they are sometimes supposed to portray a sense of foreboding and death, but it never comes across as so.
