Official Guide
(Applies to Crusader: No Remorse. May apply to Crusader 2)

Crusader: No Remorse requires both a quick hand in combat and a quick mind to discover other options. ORIGIN's Official Guide to Crusader: No Remorse helps optimize both, with hints and clues for puzzle-solving. In the savage future world of Crusader, a rebel commando needs all the edge he can find. This book provides that edge.
  • The only official, authorized guide to Crusader: No Remorse. No other book goes straight to the source for the whole story!
  • Full-color maps taken directly from the on-screen art.
  • Advice to give the Silencer the best odds in those really tough firefights.
  • Hints for each mission, for players who just need a little help to figure things out for themselves.
  • A complete walkthrough of each mission, showing the most efficient way to win.
  • Complete game stats for every enemy, robot, weapon and item in the game.
  • Exclusive interviews with the creators and developers of the game, plus background material on the setting and characters of Crusader.
