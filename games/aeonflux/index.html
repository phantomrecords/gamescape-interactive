Coming Late 1997

Introducing MTV's® one and only leather-clad heroine...

Become MTV's Æon FluxTM through 6 action-packed missions in a high-stakes world where the fight for survival startles you at every turn. Solve intricate puzzles to stop Trevor Goodchild's dangerous schemes. Not only will you have to defeat numerous enemies, you'll clone yourself, outwit the blue "zombies," and master a mysterious time manipulation technology to capture the elusive Demiurge.

Ever want to dress sexy and shoot people? Scratch that itch here!
