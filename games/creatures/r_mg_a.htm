So what is unique about playing with little, electronically-generated hatchlings? "It is the artificial intelligence and subsequent element of unpredictability," answers Leps. "You can tell a Creature that it needs to eat.

It looks at you, looks at the food and then does eat it. "Yet just when you think that you have taught the Creature to sit still when you tell it to, it ups and heads off on its own mission.

However, according to Nu Metro Multimedia, the real fun starts when a Creature reaches adolescence. "You will be simply unable to separate male and female teenage Creatures with overactive hormones," says Leps. "And after a pregnancy which lasts

about 2 human hours, you'll have another little Creature running around.

There is a way to get rid of a creature: e-mail it to someone on the Internet, and let them look after the damn thing.
