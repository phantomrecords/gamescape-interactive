Potions and Elixirs
Potion of Full Healing - Restores all of your Hit Points.
Potion of Full Mana - Restores all of your Mana Points.
Potion of Healing - Restores some of your Hit Points.
Potion of Mana - Restores some of your Mana Points.
Elixir of Clumsiness - Reduces Dexterity by 1-5.
Elixir of Dexterity - Increase Dexterity by 1-5.
Elixir of Disillusion - Reduces Magic by 1-5.
Elixir of Magic - Increases Magic by 1-5.
Elixir of Strength - Increases Strength by 1-5.
Elixir of Weakness - Decreases Strength by 1-5.
