Online Ordering Is Safe!
The New York Times had this to say about online credit card transactions:

"Consumers fearful of sending credit card numbers over the Internet should look again at transactions they already take for granted." Lewis argues that "sending a credit card number to an electronic merchant over the Internet is probably the safest way to make such a transaction. After all," Lewis writes, "consumers routinely take much greater risks when they hand their cards to waiters, read the number over the phone, or send such information via fax machine." By comparison, Internet transactions are much harder to intercept and nearly impossible to decode.

NYT Columnist Peter H. Lewis (21 Nov 1996)
