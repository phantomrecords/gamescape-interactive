Spells
Apocalypse - This spell, when cast, ignites the surrounding area into a ball of fire.
Blood Boil - Casting this spell allows you to become 'charged.' When creatures touch you they exploxde.
Blood Ritual - When you cast this spell your Health is converted to Mana.
Chain Lightning - This spell is similar to the Lightning spell, however, this spell bounces from one assailant to another.
Doom Serpents - This spell summons two snakes to emerge from the ground and attack a monster that you choose.
Etherealize - Invoking the Etherealize spell alters the state of the recipient causing them to become solid.
Fireball - This spell, although similar in appearance to the Firebolt spell, has impressive results upon impact.
Firebolt - When casting this spell the user can select a single creature to target with a small ball of flame.
Firewall - Casting this spell creates a brief wall of fire connecting the nearest two structures around you.
Flame Wave - Casting this spell invokes a wall of fire rising from the ground and proceeding outward.
Golem - When successfully casting this spell the user can target a single creature turning them to stone.
Guardian - Casting this potent spell summons a creature who acts like a Sentinel but can cast the Fireball spell.
Healing - Heals a certain number of hit points depending on your experience level.
Identify - When you invoke this spell you can determine an item's properties.
Infravision - Casting this spell allows you to see all living creatures outside your normal sight of vision.
Invisibility - This spell allows you to become transparent to all creatures based upon sight.
Lightning - This spell generates a bolt of lightning which can affect multiple creatures, provided they are in a straight line.
Mana Shield - Using this unique speel allows you to absorb damage through your Mana instead of Health.
Nova - This spell creates a ring of lightning around the caster then expands outward until hitting a solid structure.
Phasing - When the user casts this spell s/he is randomly transported to a previously explored area.
Repair - This particular spell allows the user to partially repair any inventory item, however, each use decreases durability.
Sentinal - Casting this spell summons an eyeball which uses the Firebolt spell to guard the area against advancing opponents.
Staff Recharge - This spell is similar to the Repair spell except will recharge a magical staff.
Stone Curse - This spell temporarily transforms any living creature to stone making them susceptible to attack.
Teleport - The teleport spell allows you to transport to the location of the cursor.
Town Portal - When casting this spell the user creates an oval shaped 'gate' which transports you to town. The portal remains active until you use enter it again.
Trap Disarm - This spell allows the caster to disarm any trap which may have been placed on a chest, sarcophagus, door, or other item.
Unknown - When the user cast this spell all living creatures within an eight square radius are destroyed.
