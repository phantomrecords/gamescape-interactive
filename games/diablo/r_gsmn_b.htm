Storyline:

The story borrows heavily from Christian mythology, and this gives the game a great sense of urgency and epic quality. There is some

character interaction-you can actually get little quests from the people you meet in Tristram. Don't expect the character depth of an Ultima, though. The characters are fairly stock, and their voice work is a little crude. Your conversations are restricted to a very narrow set of topics, or simple business transactions. And all they seem to do is stand in the doorways of their homes or shops, waiting for you to drop by.

Diablo has one killer opening movie. And there are

some strange plot developments which are surprisingly unnerving. Now what would demons want with the sign of a local tavern?
