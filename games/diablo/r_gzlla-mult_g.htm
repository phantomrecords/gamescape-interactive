The other kind of leech in Diablo is the thief. Certain individuals are too inept to get treasure from the dungeon themselves, so they will steal from other characters, usually by ripping someone off in a bargain or stealing items from a player's corpse. If you are ever trading items, make sure you and the other individual

make a game with a password, so another player cannot simply jump in, take the goods, and run. In addition, it is best to stand on opposite sides of the river near the witch if you do not know or trust the individual. In this manner, you can drop the items, ensure they are correct by passing the cursor over them and verifying them, and then both crossing the bridge to the other side. This way you can watch the other individual to ensure they don't pick up something and run.
