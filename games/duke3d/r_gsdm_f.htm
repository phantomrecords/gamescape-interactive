To sum up, Duke Nukem 3D is top stuff. If you liked Doom, Hexen, or if you've even got a drop of gung-ho'ness in your body, get Duke 3D. You'll not be disappointed. And if you are, it's not my fault. I only work here.

Summary

Appeal -Doom fans, anyone with a taste for the violent.. Tarantino fans, perhaps...

Originality & Storyline - Quel Storyline? It's a Doom

style game, but with enough innovation and excellence to make it stand out.

Graphics & Video - Very good... even in standard VGA mode. Lots of big explosions, and neat touches. The bosses are particularly nasty

Audio - Good - lots of screams, bangs, whooshes, and speech.

Longevity - Long. High replay value, to get all those secrets you missed, and then there's a level editor.... I sense

a flurry of incoming Duke levels.

Presentation - Standard for this sort of game.. a la Doom.

Packaging & Docs - Okay... a fairly thin in-cd manual, with other docs included on CD.

Bugs & Problems - None noticed.

PROS - Engaging, fun to play, and very very violent.

CONS - Not enough different monsters?

-Games Domain
